Project Overview

This project is a Java-based quantum computing simulator that demonstrates fundamental quantum concepts and two famous quantum algorithms.

  • Quantum States
  • Complex Amplitudes
  • Hadamard Gates
  • Quantum Measurement
  • Deutsch-Jozsa Algorithm
  • Bernstein-Vazirani Algorithm

Why Quantum Computing?

Quantum computers use superposition and interference to solve some problems more efficiently than classical computers.
